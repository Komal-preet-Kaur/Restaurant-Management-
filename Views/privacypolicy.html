Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to DineDelight! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website to explore restaurants, book tables, and engage with our services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you make a booking, register an account, or contact us, we collect your name, email, phone number, and any special requests.
  • Usage Data: We collect details about your interactions with our website, such as page views, clicks, and time spent on different sections.
  • Cookies: To enhance your experience, we use cookies that help us track preferences and website performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process restaurant reservations and provide a seamless booking experience.
  • To send confirmations, updates, and promotional offers.
  • To improve website functionality and user experience.
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or trade your personal data. However, we may share information with:

  • Partner Restaurants: To confirm reservations and special requests.
  • Service Providers: For email notifications and website analytics.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@dinedelight.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and significant updates will be communicated via email.
